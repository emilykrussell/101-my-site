A little bit about me.

My start in tech was by happenstance - working in sales for the graphic industry, I was exposed to design and instantly knew I needed to learn the art. After some time, I decided to learn development as a hobby, but quickly grew to much more than that.

I am fascinated by simple, classic design styles and I strive to create new interpretations of those inspirations every day. The ability to create design and bring it to life through development is my passion.

When I'm not deep in design or working on code, you can find me at home in Carolina with my husband and our two pups.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
